The Protocol

How Nythera works

Four steps to make your seed phrase undeniably secure and recoverable — without ever storing it whole in one place.

01

Enter Seed Phrase

Input your 12 or 24-word phrase locally in your browser. It never leaves your device unencrypted.

02

CDR Shard Split

Nythera uses CDR encryption to split your phrase into threshold shards — any subset can reconstruct, none alone is useful.

03

Distribute to Guardians

Each shard is sent to a trusted guardian — family, friend, or institutional key — via encrypted on-chain attestation.

04

Recover Anytime

When needed, guardians submit their shards. Nythera reconstructs and hands back full wallet access — seamlessly.

The Engine

Built on CDR

Nythera is powered by CDR (Content-Defined Recovery) — a cryptographic standard that makes split secret storage verifiable on-chain. Your shards are not just encrypted; they carry proof of authenticity and threshold logic baked in at the protocol level.

🔐

Threshold Secret Sharing

2-of-3, 3-of-5, or custom quorums. Recovery only possible with your defined minimum of guardian shards.

⛓️

On-Chain Attestation

Shard delivery and guardian acceptance are recorded as verifiable on-chain proofs — no central server required.

⏰

Inactivity Triggers

Set time-based unlock conditions. If you don't check in for 12 months, guardians are automatically notified to begin recovery.

🌐

Chain Agnostic

Works across EVM chains, Solana, and beyond. Your recovery vault is not tied to any single network.

// Nythera CDR shard creation
const vault = await nythera.createVault({
  seedPhrase: userInput,
  threshold: 2,
  guardians: [
    "0xAlice...",
    "0xBob...",
    "0xCarol..."
  ],
  inactivityDays: 365
});

// Shards distributed. Phrase never stored whole.
console.log(vault.onChainProof);
// → "ipfs://Qm4n7h3r4..."

// Recovery — any 2 guardians submit:
const recovered = await nythera.recover({
  shards: [aliceShard, bobShard],
  proof: vault.onChainProof
});
